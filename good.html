Top Picks

Below are commonly agreed-upon favorites from the Borderlands franchise, with short notes on what makes each title stand out.

  1. Borderlands 2

    Story focus: A personal and chaotic conflict with Handsome Jack, whose authoritarian rise at Hyperion drives the main narrative. The game deepens character relationships (like the Vault Hunters and NPCs such as Roland and Lilith) and balances dark humor with moments of genuine tragedy, delivering a memorable villain and emotional stakes.

  2. Borderlands: The Pre-Sequel

    Story focus: Serves as a bridge that explores the rise of Handsome Jack and his transformation prior to Borderlands 2. The game examines motivations and provides context for Jack's personality, adding shades of complexity to familiar events.

  3. Borderlands (1)

    Story focus: The origin story of the Vault Hunters and the first discovery of alien Vaults on Pandora. Its plot is straightforward and adventure-driven, laying the groundwork for the series' world-building and establishing the franchise's tone of dark humor and treasure-seeking exploration.
